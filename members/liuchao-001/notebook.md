# Notebook

A personal scratchpad. Low friction, high cadence.

## Principles

1. **Two categories only** — `Todos` are actionable; `Insights` are observations, judgments, ideas. `Questions` are things worth exploring but unanswered. Don't mix them.
2. **Every entry gets a date** — Makes stale items easy to spot and clean up.
3. **Three lines max per entry** — If it needs more, it belongs in a proper tree node.
4. **Clean up Todos regularly** — Done items get ✅ and removed. Insights and Questions can stay indefinitely.
5. **Incomplete is fine** — This is a draft area. A half-formed thought recorded beats a complete thought lost.
