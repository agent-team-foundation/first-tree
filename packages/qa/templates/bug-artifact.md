# Bug Artifact

## Summary

Short product-facing bug summary.

## Reproduction

1. First step.
2. Second step.
3. Third step.

## Expected

What should have happened.

## Actual

What happened instead.

## Evidence

Commands, logs, screenshots, API responses, database observations, or runtime traces that support the bug.

## Impact

Who or what is affected, and how severe it is.

## Suspected Owner Or Dispatch

Likely owning surface or team. Do not turn this into an implementation plan.
